## üìà Valuation Comparison: Sell vs. Service

### **Sell Now (Pre-Revenue):**
- **Valuation:** $8M - $15M
- **Pros:** Immediate payout, no operational risk
- **Cons:** Miss future upside, lower valuation

### **Sell After 1 Year ($1M ARR):**
- **Valuation:** $12M - $20M
- **Pros:** Higher valuation, proven traction
- **Cons:** Operational costs, execution risk

### **Sell After 3 Years ($5M ARR):**
- **Valuation:** $40M - $60M
- **Pros:** Maximum valuation, proven business
- **Cons:** 3 years of work, operational complexity

### **Build to $10M+ ARR:**
- **Valuation:** $100M - $300M+
- **Pros:** Massive upside, market leadership
- **Cons:** 5-7 years, significant capital needed
