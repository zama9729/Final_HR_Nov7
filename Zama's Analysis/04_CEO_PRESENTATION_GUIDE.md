## Q&A Preparation

### Anticipated Questions & Answers

**Q: Why should we invest in this vs. buying an existing solution?**
> A: "We've already built it. The investment is in enhancement, not creation. We have full control, no vendor lock-in, and can customize without restrictions. Plus, we have unique AI features competitors don't offer."

**Q: What's the biggest risk?**
> A: "Market adoption. But we have strong competitive advantages - cost, speed, modern tech. The market is large ($4.2B mid-market), and we're targeting a specific segment where we have clear advantages."

**Q: Can we compete with ServiceNow?**
> A: "In mid-market, absolutely. We're 50-70% cheaper with comparable features. For enterprise, we'd need more time, but our architecture scales. The key is we don't need to beat ServiceNow everywhere - just in our target segment."

**Q: What about support and maintenance?**
> A: "Our modern architecture means lower maintenance costs. Self-hosted option reduces support burden. We can build a support team as we scale, or partner with managed service providers."

**Q: Timeline seems aggressive. Is it realistic?**
> A: "Yes. We've already built the foundation. Phase 1 features are well-defined. We have the architecture in place. The timeline is aggressive but achievable with focused execution."
